# Superstore Sales Dashboard - Future Interns DS Track

## 📌 Project Overview
Analysis of business sales data to identify revenue trends,
top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional 
performance using Power BI.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Microsoft Excel

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- Revenue trend over time (Line Chart)
- Top 10 selling products (Bar Chart)
- Sales by category (Donut Chart)
- Regional performance (Bar Chart)
- KPI Cards (Total Revenue, Profit Margin, Top Sales, Top Profits)
- Interactive slicers (Year, Region)

## 💡 Key Insights
- Revenue shows a consistent upward trend over time
- Zebra ZM400 is the top selling product by revenue
- Technology is the highest revenue category
- West region leads in sales, South is the lowest performer
- Overall profit margin is 12.47%, above retail industry average

## ✅ Recommendations
- Invest more resources in replicating West region strategy 
  across other regions
- Investigate and address why South region underperforms
- Focus marketing and inventory on Technology category
- Review discounting strategy to protect profit margins
